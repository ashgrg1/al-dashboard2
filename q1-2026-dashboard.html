Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities & Threats
Generated from the Q1 data — figures shown are directly from your numbers.

Strengths

  • March surge: revenue hit $284,442, up +18.2% over February and the strongest month, led by Packaged Beverages and Foodservice.
  • Foodservice engine: $107,298 (13.8% of sales) from just 2 subcategories; the Beef & Bean Burrito alone did $21,026 on 7,102 units — your #1 true retail item.
  • Beverage anchor: Packaged Beverages led at $163,318 (21%), with energy/alt drinks the top subcategory at $65,638.
  • Healthy basket: avg of $4.58 per unit across 169,481 units sold.

Weaknesses

  • February slump: the only down month — $240,667, off -3.9% from January, with most departments softening.
  • Inflated topline: the "Expense" bucket of $55,768 is mostly pass-through Cash App card activations ($33,755) and ice — near-zero margin, so true retail strength is below the headline.
  • Dead weight SKUs: Wine ($2,023) and Frozen Carbonated ($672) barely move — shelf/space worth reviewing.
  • Narrow Foodservice menu: huge dept, only 2 subcategories — fragile if a top item stocks out.

Opportunities

  • Grow the daypart: breakfast items (croissant $4,977, biscuit $3,225) and chicken bites show demand — extend Foodservice beyond burritos/flautas.
  • Ride beverage momentum: energy ($65,638) + sports drinks ($16,673) are climbing — premium cooler placement and bundles.
  • Carry March into Q2: the +18% lift is a base to defend with staffing & stock around peak demand.
  • Fountain attach: 32oz + 44oz fountain moved 7,000+ cups — pair with Foodservice combos.

Threats

  • Tobacco reliance: cigarettes + other tobacco = $134,376 (17%) against long-term decline and tightening regulation.
  • Low-margin volume: Lottery ($72,220) and card-activation revenue look big but contribute little profit and swing month to month.
  • Concentration risk: a handful of SKUs drive a large share — supply or price shocks hit hard.
  • Seasonality: the Feb dip suggests demand sensitivity that can repeat in slow stretches.
